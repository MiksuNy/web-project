# Help Connect

A community platform designed to connect people who need help with individuals who are willing to support them.

---

## Project Overview

**Help Connect** is a web platform concept aimed at creating stronger communities by making it easier for people to request help and for others to offer support. The platform focuses on accessibility, connection, and community-based assistance.

The long-term vision is to develop Help Connect into a digital platform or mobile application where people can easily find support, share resources, and help one another.

---

## Origin of the Idea

The original idea for **Help Connect** was created by **Minoo Yaghoubi**.

The inspiration for this concept comes from Minoo’s personal experiences in her home country. After becoming a mother, she often needed help and realized that many support services were limited or difficult to access. This experience led to the idea of creating a platform that could connect people who need help with those who are willing to provide it.

Later, while studying at **Metropolia University of Applied Sciences**, Minoo shared this idea with her university web project group. The idea was then selected and developed as the foundation of the team’s course project.
